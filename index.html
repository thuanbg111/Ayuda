AYUDA

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our mission is to make and distribute masks and other essential protective items to prevent the spread of COVID, while educating the public on community health and safety.

What We Do

Hands‑on production and distribution of protective supplies.

  • Volunteer‑led mask making
  • Distribution to clinics, shelters, and families
  • Public health education and safety tips

Mask Making Workshops

Learn safe patterns and techniques; contribute to community supply.

Distribution & Outreach

Deliver masks and kits to frontline partners and neighbors.

Health Education

Simple, multilingual materials that promote prevention and community care.
